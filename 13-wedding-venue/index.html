§ 01 · Three spaces

Three rooms, one estate.

Ceremony

The rose garden

Sixty by ninety feet, oak arbor, seats up to 220 in wooden folding chairs. West-facing — the ceremony is planned for the hour before civil dusk.

Cocktails

The olive terrace

Flagstone terrace overlooking the vineyard. Fifty olive trees planted 1974, string lights, two bars during high season.

Reception

The stone barn

1904 stone-and-timber barn, restored 2019. Seats 200 rounds or 260 long tables. Full commercial kitchen, in-house sound.

§ 02 · The day

A template for the day.

2:00 pm

Vendor arrival & setup

Our on-site coordinator and grounds team receive every vendor. Load-in ends by 3:15 sharp — we run the door.

4:30 pm

Guest arrival & welcome

Guests are directed to the olive terrace. A signature spritz and a small welcome table on the north lawn.

5:15 pm

Ceremony

Under the oak arbor, west-facing, in the rose garden. Photographs are made in the last half-hour of good light.

6:00 pm

Cocktails & canapés

On the olive terrace. Two bars, walk-around canapés, a raw bar in high season.

7:30 pm

Dinner in the stone barn

Long tables or rounds, service under the timber roof. Toasts around dessert, coffee and cognac at the door.

10:30 pm

Dancing & farewell

Music to eleven, farewell circle in the rose garden with sparklers, on-site until midnight to close.
