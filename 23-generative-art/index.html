§ 02 · About the piece

What you are looking at.

A Perlin-noise flow-field with a seedable pseudo-random generator (mulberry32). Each visit produces a unique piece — the seed is stamped in the corner and encoded in the URL, so any seed is rediscoverable.

The piece is generated live in your browser. There is no server render. Prints are captured at 4× the on-screen resolution and printed on archival cotton by our press in Riga.

An edition of 1. Each seed is retired at first order.
