§ 02 · Process

Four stages. No shortcuts.

01

Intake

Photo record of every panel + interior. Paint depth measured with electronic gauge. Written intake report emailed before we begin.

02

Correction

Wash, decontaminate, clay, correct. Between one and three passes with diminishing abrasives, room-temp climate-controlled.

03

Protection

Ceramic layer applied panel by panel in twelve-hour intervals. PPF applied by a single technician with a laser template.

04

Handoff

Photo record of every finished panel. Written care card + a bottle of maintenance shampoo. Ninety-day check-in wash, complimentary.
