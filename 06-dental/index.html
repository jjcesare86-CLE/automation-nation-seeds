§ 02 · Approach

We run ahead of schedule.

Every appointment begins fifteen minutes ahead of its listed time. Every quote is written before we begin — no verbal upsells at the chair. Every visit ends with a printed itemized invoice you can read at the front desk.

We accept most PPO insurance, but we do not have a preferred one. Direct-pay patients get a five percent same-day discount — not because we mean to hide the sticker, but because it costs us less to process.
