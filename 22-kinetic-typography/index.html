§ 02 · Manifesto

Type is infrastructure.

A wordmark is not a picture. It is the load-bearing beam of an identity, and beam design has been abandoned to the machine.

We build every letter like it will be watched at 60 frames a second by a person on a train who is unhappy about their week.

The variable axis is not decoration. It is the difference between a static company and one that can be woken up.

§ 03 · Studio

Write the studio.

We take on three projects a year. Every engagement begins with a two-week paid discovery — no discovery, no proposal, no engagement letter. Founders and design leads write to us directly.
