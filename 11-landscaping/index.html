§ 02 · Process

Four steps. Two to eighteen months.

01

Walk

A ninety-minute site visit with our lead designer and a soil sample kit. No fee. If we're not a fit, we say so on the walk.

02

Draw

A three-drawing set: site plan, planting plan, section. Delivered as a bound A3 folio. Two revisions included.

03

Build

Hardscape first, plant last. Our crew of six is on-site every day the project runs. We do not sub the planting.

04

Return

Free two-year check-ins each spring and fall. Guaranteed replant on any plant we specified that dies year one.

§ 03 · Design intake

A short letter before we walk.
