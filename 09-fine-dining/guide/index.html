Rebrand Guide · Site 09 · Fine Dining

Vespertine Room

A single-page tasting menu presented like a printed program. GT Sectra italic serif, aubergine palette, cursor-following radial spotlight, three plated-course photographs.

Rebrand in 15 minutes

  1. Edit brand.json.
  2. Update the 5 palette CSS vars.
  3. Replace the 3 course-*.webp images (3:4 aspect).
  4. Rewrite the nine menu courses to match the client's actual program.

Signature techniques

  • Cursor-following spotlight (respects reduced motion).
  • Course cards with roman-numeral course numbers and italic serif titles.
