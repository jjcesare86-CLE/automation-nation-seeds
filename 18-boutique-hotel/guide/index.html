The Larkspur

A single-page identity for a small city hotel. Slow pan on the exterior hero, availability strip with live tonight/rate/rooms, three room types (rooms/restaurant/rooftop) each with its own image.

Rebrand in 15 minutes

  1. Edit brand.json.
  2. Update 5 palette CSS vars.
  3. Swap the three images: hero.webp (exterior), room.webp, bar.webp.
