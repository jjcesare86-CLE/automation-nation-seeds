§ Process

Four stages. Fourteen to twenty-four months.

01

Programming

Ninety-day discovery. We interview every occupant. Deliverable: a bound brief, adopted or amended by you.

02

Design

Six-month schematic + design-development phase with the architect. Every allowance itemized. Weekly review calls.

03

Build

Ten to eighteen months on site. Same construction lead every day. Change orders in writing, priced within 48 hours.

04

Handover

Written handover binder, a one-year warranty walk, and a check-in visit each October for five years.

§ Enquire

A meeting begins every commission.

Every project begins with a two-hour paid meeting between you, the architect, and our founding partner. No proposal is drafted before the meeting. If we can't sit down within four weeks, we say so before you send this.
