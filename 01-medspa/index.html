§ 01 · The practice

Every face has a natural key. Our job is to hear it, and to work under it — not against it. Nothing here reshapes what you already sound like.

The intake

Ninety minutes, seated, no clipboards. We take photographs in three light temperatures, listen to your last decade of skincare, and — if it's the right visit — we begin.

The follow-through

Every treatment is paired with a two-week check-in call at the same time on the same weekday. No portal messages. A human at a desk, with your file open.

The room

One treatment room, north-facing, wool floor, a chair that reclines from vertical, and a window that stays open in summer. We built the space around the light.
