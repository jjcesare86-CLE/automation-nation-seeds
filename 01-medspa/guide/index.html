Rebrand Guide · Site 01 · Med Spa / Aesthetics

Lumen Aesthetics

An editorial, "afternoon light" med spa. Sits on the strength of one window-lit portrait, an ink-underlining treatment menu, and a first-visit intake form written like a personal letter.

Concept

Every page rhythm assumes the visitor is looking at their skin, not shopping. Language is hushed, first-person plural, and never uses the words "trusted," "boost," or "elevate." Fraunces italic carries emotion; Manrope carries operating information. Warm accent is used once per surface — the whole site would fall apart if the warm color were used decoratively.

What lives where

  • brand.json — brand name, address, hours, phone, email, palette, FORM_WEBHOOK_URL.
  • index.html — every editable string tagged with data-editable. Search for the attribute to find them all.
  • styles.css — five palette tokens at the top of :root. All colors reference them.
  • app.js — hero parallax, menu accordion, form submit, IntersectionObserver-driven reveals.
  • assets/hero-portrait.webp — the one image the site depends on. 16:9, subject on the left, warm-neutral negative space on the right where the headline sits.

Rebrand in 15 minutes

  1. Edit brand.json: name, address, hours, phone, palette tokens, webhook.
  2. Update the five palette tokens in styles.css :root to match.
  3. Swap assets/hero-portrait.webp. Aspect ratio 16:9, subject occupies the left third, right two-thirds are the negative space the headline sits over.
  4. Rewrite copy — every data-editable attribute names its semantic role (hero.headline, menu.01.title, etc.).
  5. Rewrite the treatment menu — the five rows are the priced-and-timed services the client actually offers. Keep the pattern (duration, from/at price, description, follow-up cadence).
  6. Rename Dr. Ravel in the letter block to the practice's actual practitioner of record.

Signature techniques

  • Menu row ink underline — hover or open a row and the warm accent underline draws from left to right at 600ms with cubic-bezier(.2,.9,.2,1).
  • Hero parallax — image translates 4% over the hero's scroll length, respects reduced motion.
  • Header dissolve — the top bar picks up its bone-colored fill only after the hero has scrolled out, so it never fights the image.
  • Reveal stagger — philosophy, journal, and menu rows enter the viewport at a 50ms cadence.
  • The letter — the intake form CTA is Send the letter. Small, deliberate. Do not change this without also softening the copy.

Copy voice cheat sheet

  • First-person plural. "We work in afternoon light."
  • Never "boost," "achieve," "reveal your best skin." Say the specific outcome, in specific units.
  • Prices are references. Every price ends in a plus, or a single number. Never a range with a dash — a dash reads like a haggle.
  • Follow-ups are named. "Complimentary at 14 days." "Return at 4 weeks."
  • The practitioner signs the letter. If your client isn't comfortable signing, cut the block — don't fake it.
