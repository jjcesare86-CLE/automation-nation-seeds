We manage fewer relationships, on purpose.

Sonderberg Advisors is a four-partner independent private wealth practice. We are fee-only, no commissions, no revenue from any product provider. We take on eight new client relationships a year and cap the firm at fifty-two families. This is a design constraint, not a marketing one.

§ 01 · Philosophy

We plan the next fifty years, not the next quarter.

Every client relationship begins with a five-hour financial architecture session, held in our library, in person, with all four partners present. We do not delegate the initial reading. If we cannot articulate the plan back to you within two weeks, the fee for the session is refunded and no engagement letter is signed.

Portfolios are built around after-tax, after-cash-flow objectives, not benchmark comparison. We publish a private quarterly letter to clients only, discussing what changed, what did not change, and why. There is no marketing team.
