Rebrand Guide · Site 16 · Wealth Management

Sonderberg Advisors

A private wealth firm's site set in a data-visualization sensibility — thin serif, mono captions, an SVG performance chart that self-draws (2.4s cubic-bezier(.85,0,.15,1)) at load, and a fully-scoped "no marketing team" voice.

Rebrand in 15 minutes

  1. Edit brand.json.
  2. Update 5 palette custom properties.
  3. Rewrite the four partner cards (initials shown, not photos — this is deliberate for a fiduciary firm).
  4. Adjust the path.line and path.bench in the SVG chart to represent the client's real illustrative composite. Keep the disclaimer.

Signature techniques

  • Self-drawing SVG performance chart with stroke-dasharray animation.
  • Initials-only partners (no photography — reads as regulated / senior).
  • Mono body copy for operating information, Fraunces italic for human moments.
