Rebrand Guide · Site 17 · Charter

Meridian Charters

A global private jet + yacht charter brokerage. Zero-asset canvas world map with traveling route arcs, a live "fleet status" ticker, and standing-pair route cards.

Rebrand in 15 minutes

  1. Edit brand.json.
  2. Update the 5 palette custom properties.
  3. Update the six fleet cards to match the client's real inventory.
  4. Update the five standing-pair route cards.
  5. Adjust the ticker entries to reflect real aircraft callsigns / states.

Signature techniques

  • Canvas dot-map with continent gating + traveling quadratic-bezier route arcs.
  • Live fleet-status ticker (marquee).
  • Zero generated assets — the whole hero is procedural.
