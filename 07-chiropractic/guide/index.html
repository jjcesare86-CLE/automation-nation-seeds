Rebrand Guide · Site 07 · Chiropractic

Meridian Spinal Care

An earth-palette chiropractic practice that reads sports-medicine but feels calm. The spine SVG in the hero rotates as the page scrolls; individual vertebrae highlight as the reader arrives at the matching service card (Cervical, Thoracic, Lumbar).

Rebrand in 15 minutes

  1. Edit brand.json — name, phone, address, palette.
  2. Update the 5 CSS custom properties in the site's :root.
  3. Rewrite copy tagged with data-editable.
  4. Adjust the three service cards to match the client's real pricing structure.

Signature techniques

  • SVG spine, scroll-linked rotation (0° → 30° across the page height).
  • IntersectionObserver highlights the correct vertebrae + label when each service card is on-screen.
  • Single-file portable — no separate .css/.js.
