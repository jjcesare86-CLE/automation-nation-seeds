§ 01 · What we do

Four services. No middlemen.

01

Full tear-off & installation

We strip to the deck, replace rot, and install architectural shingles with a synthetic underlayment rated 90°F to −40°F. Typical single-family finish: 2–3 days.

Lifetime workmanship + 50-yr shingle
02

Storm damage & insurance claims

A licensed adjuster walks the roof with your carrier's rep. We photograph, document, and file. You pay your deductible. We handle the rest.

No out-of-pocket beyond deductible · guaranteed
03

Repairs & patching

Missing shingles, ice-dam damage, flashing at chimneys and valleys, dead-valley reworks. We fix, we don't upsell. Repair-only visits welcomed.

2-year sealed-in-place workmanship
04

Gutters, soffit & fascia

Seamless 5" and 6" aluminum in twelve stock colors, half-round copper on request. Gutter guards installed with clip fasteners — no fascia damage.

10-yr no-leak · manufacturer + our own
