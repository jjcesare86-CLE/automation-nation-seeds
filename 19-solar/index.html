§ 01 · How it works

Three steps. One truck.

01

Free savings estimate

Send your last twelve utility bills. We size an array and send back a written estimate — panel count, kWh output, payback period, and net cost after federal + AZ credit.

02

Signed proposal, permit pulled

Once you sign, we pull your city permit, coordinate the AHJ inspection, and file the interconnection request with SRP or APS.

03

Install in one day

Our crew installs the racking, panels, and inverter in a single day. Utility PTO (permission to operate) follows within four weeks. You are net-metered from day one of PTO.

§ 03 · Financing

Three ways. All owned.

Cash

The best net-present-value. Federal 30% ITC + AZ 25% state credit stack. Typical payback 5.5 yrs.

Solar loan · 20 yr

Fixed rate through our credit-union partner. No dealer fees. Typical payment lower than the utility bill it replaces.

HELOC / cash-out refi

Best rate, tax-deductible interest if your borrow is under the home-improvement cap. We refer you to a broker we've used for eight years.
