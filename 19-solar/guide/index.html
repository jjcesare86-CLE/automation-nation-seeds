Rebrand Guide · Site 19 · Solar

Sunward Solar

A residential solar installer with a scientific-instrument palette. Isometric SVG roof self-installs panels one at a time in the hero. Working savings estimator with three inputs computes real numbers live.

Rebrand in 15 minutes

  1. Edit brand.json.
  2. Update 5 palette CSS vars.
  3. Adjust the estimator constants in the calc() function for the client's market (utility rate, credit stack).

Signature techniques

  • SVG roof isometric with panels appearing one by one at staggered animation delays.
  • Live estimator that recomputes yearly savings, array size, payback period, and lifetime savings on input.
