§ 01 · Services

What a Cedarwood visit looks like.

Wellness & preventive

Annual exams, vaccines, spay/neuter, dentals, and lifetime care plans for dogs and cats. All appointments run 45 minutes.

Diagnostics & imaging

In-house digital radiology, ultrasound, and CBC/chem panels. Results in twenty minutes for most panels.

Soft-tissue surgery

Mass removals, ACL repairs, foreign body surgery. Board-certified surgeon on the roster, Wed & Fri.

Dentistry

Full-mouth digital x-ray, ultrasonic scale + polish, extractions. Every dental starts with a phone call from the doctor.

End-of-life care

In-clinic and in-home euthanasia. Grief counselor on call. Cremation and paw-print keepsake included at no charge.

Same-day urgent care

Two same-day urgent-care slots held daily. Call by 10 AM; we'll fit you in that afternoon.
