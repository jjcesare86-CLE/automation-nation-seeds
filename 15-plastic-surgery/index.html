§ 02 · The practice

Dr. Ines Ravel, MD, FACS

Founded Ravel Surgical in 2013. Board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. Fellowship trained in microsurgery at Memorial Sloan-Kettering.

Practice caps at fourteen surgical patients per month. Every consultation is ninety minutes, seated. Every surgical date includes a first-week post-op home visit performed by the surgeon.
